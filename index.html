Events Map App

Find your next event here!

Simply pick a category of meetup that interests you, type in your location, and enter the maximum distance you would like to travel (in miles!).

You'll receive a map of all the events with markers and information on each event.

This website is made possible by meetup.com and google.com APIs. All events are hosted on meetup.com and clicking on the events will take you to the events specific page on meetup.com
